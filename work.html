My Accomplishments
The past 17 years of my life have given me many experiences and opportunities. On this page, you will find information about notable achievments and my skills.
As an experienced assistant nurse at the inner-city Jackson medical clinic based in Greenwood, I have served countless patients with life-threatening conditions. Developing connections with the individuals I served, I have been able to build close relationships and fond memories. Although my career at the clinic has consisted of numerous positive interactions, I have definitely encountered stressful situations. Handling calls after calls, learning each time from the one prior, my fast learning skills allowed me to prosper even more. One of my patients, Arvin, a homeless black man, later died after being struck by a car driven by an angry racist. Actually, it was me who had been hit by the car at first, but Arvin was kind enough to try and assist me. The experience taught me about who our patients are, and why we do what we do to help them. My medical skills are an important facet of my character, and my experiences with Arvin and at the clinic show how hard-working and dedicated I am towards helping others.
